TERMS & CONDITIONS
The terms and conditions mentioned herewith are applicable for reservations made on the worldwide Heritance Hotels and Resorts website hotel reservation system. This includes reservations at Heritance Tea Factory and Sentido Heritance Negombo. These terms and conditions do not apply to travel agent or operator bookings; for which the applicable policies and procedures will apply.
Cancellation Policy
If you are unable to travel or utilise the accommodation and services previously confirmed via email, cancellation fees may apply. If cancelled or modified three (03) nights prior to the date of arrival, reservation amount excluding the bank transaction fee will be refunded. If cancelled or modified within three (03) days or in the event of a no-show, full amount of the reservation will be charged.

A change in the length of stay or dates of your reservation is considered a revision. Revisions are subject to availability and rate changes; and may also constitute a cancellation. In case of shortened length of stay, the cancellation policy will apply for the cancelled nights. If a booking deposit is to be refunded (at the discretion of the Management), please note that any credit card surcharge or currency fluctuations are unable to be refunded.

Cancellation, early departure, and no-show fees incurred are the sole responsibility of the client and will be charged accordingly as follows:
Early departures or no-shows
A fee equivalent to 100% per night plus taxes for early departures applies. In the event of a no-show (i.e. if you neither use nor cancel your reservation), a fee equivalent to the value of the entire stay plus taxes and one-way transfer will be incurred. Early departures or cancellations due to inclement weather are fully chargeable.
Payments
As Gazetted and made mandatory by the Monetary Board of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka, we are compelled to charge all payments from persons residing outside of Sri Lanka, only in foreign currency. More details at https://bit.ly/3uHN1ZZ.
All bank transaction fees are non-refundable under any circumstance.
Please present the same credit card used to guarantee your booking when checking-in / making payment at the resort. If you are making payment using another cardholders credit card, please contact the property as the property share the required documentation for same.

For your data's safety, please use our official channels under domains: heritancehotels.com, adaaran.com.mv, turyaahotels.com, and aitkenspence.lk. Always confirm the legitimacy of any communication before disclosing information or making payments to third parties.
Rate changes
Please be advised that service charges, fees and taxes are subject to change at any time without prior notice in accordance with the law. The prevailing tax rates at the time of stay will be charged.

Other Information
Child and Infant Policy:
Junior guests under the age of two (2) years are considered Infants, while those between the ages of two (2) and eleven (11.99) years are considered Children.
Subject to the maximum occupancy applicable by room, child rates will apply for accommodation. Appropriate child rates in accordance with the resort will also apply for transfers, special F&B events and other.
Minimum age:
There must be at least one guest of minimum 18-years of age, With a valid credit card, present upon check-in at the resort. This guest must be holding the payment for the travelling party against this credit card.
